Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Surplus collects, uses, and protects information submitted through LiveInSurplus.com.

Information we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect your name, email address, optional response about what you want help with, signup source, consent date, and basic technical information used to protect the form from abuse.

How we use it

  • To save and manage your place on the Surplus waitlist.
  • To send your signup confirmation, launch announcements, and relevant Surplus updates.
  • To understand what future members want Surplus to teach and provide.
  • To prevent spam, fraud, and misuse of the website.

Service providers

We use trusted providers to operate this website and waitlist, including Render for hosting, Supabase for database storage, and Resend for email delivery. These providers process information only as needed to provide their services.

Your choices

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe option included in those messages. You may also ask us to access, correct, or delete your waitlist information.

Contact

For privacy requests, email hello@liveinsurplus.com.

Updates

We may update this policy as Surplus adds new services. The latest version will remain available on this page with its effective date.
